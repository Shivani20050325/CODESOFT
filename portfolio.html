Merchant wallet with Mobile payment

A merchant wallet, also known as a digital or e-wallet, is a secure virtual container for storing payment methods that can be used for mobile and online payments. Merchants can integrate merchant wallets into their point-of-sale (POS) systems or e-commerce sites to allow customers to pay without having to present a card or enter payment details. Merchant wallets can offer a variety of features, including: Payment methods: Merchants can accept a variety of payment methods, such as credit cards, debit cards, and digital currencies. Value-added services: Merchant wallets can offer additional services, such as loyalty programs, product recommendations, and geolocation. Seamless checkout: Merchant wallets can provide a seamless checkout experience for customers.
